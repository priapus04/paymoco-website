About PayMo Co.

Payment technology built for growing businesses

PayMo Co., Inc. is a payment technology provider connecting merchants to payment rails across the Philippines and Southeast Asia. We make accepting and moving money simple for small and medium businesses — the engine of the regional economy.

We bring the channels you sell through and the networks your customers pay with into one connection, so you can focus on running your business instead of stitching together payment tools.

We deliver this in partnership with established infrastructure and licensed operators across the region, pairing their reach with technology designed around how SME merchants actually work.

PayMo Co., Inc. is in the process of registering as an Operator of Payment System (OPS) with the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas. Services are offered in partnership with licensed providers.
